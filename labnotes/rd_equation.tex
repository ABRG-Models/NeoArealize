This results from a meeting between Stuart and Seb on 20th September
2018. The equation is a system of n genes and gives the rate of change

\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
  \frac{\delta f_i(\mathbf{x})}{\delta t}=&+\sum\nolimits_j\alpha_{i,j}\nabla\cdot(f_i(\mathbf{x})\nabla f_j(\mathbf{x}))\\
  & +\sum\nolimits_{j\neq i}\beta_{i,j}\nabla\cdot(f_j(\mathbf{x})\nabla f_i(\mathbf{x}))\\
  & +\sum\nolimits_j\gamma_{i,j}f_j(\mathbf{x})\\
  & +q_i
\end{split}
\end{equation}
